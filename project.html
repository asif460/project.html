Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 jar of marinara sauce
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Cook ground beef and mix with marinara sauce.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg, salt, and pepper.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  5. Top with shredded mozzarella and bake at 375Â°F for 30 minutes.
  6. Let it cool before serving. Enjoy!
